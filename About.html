About me

Hello Friends!

My name is Sofiya Naaz, I am currently pursuing my third year Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science from Hyderabad. In early days of college I faced problem like how should I start learn programming,Where can I get better knowledge because formal college education is never going to be enough.We live in an era where most computer fundamentals can be learnt free on the internet. But as I started searching about these things, it really took most of the time so I just thought i should make a platform where beginners can grasp ideas about learning programming languages and more.

The right attitude to learning is to be curious.
Unfortunately, with the way colleges grade their students and how much emphasis we put into our GPAs, we tend to lose this pure and naive curiosity. Instead, we panic when everyone else seem to understand what’s going on except us. (Ironically, most of us in the class felt the same way.)
